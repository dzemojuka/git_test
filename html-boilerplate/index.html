The Most Important Heading

You can set different text importance levels using heading tags, from h1 to h6.H1 is the most important text on your page.

Search engines also see your headings as more important than your regular text.

The Second Most Important Heading

In order to make text elements, we use the paragraph tag, a letter p inside triangle brackets.

This is what the other headings look like.

Make text bold

It is possible to make text bold, by using strong element.

This will also make screen readers put some importance to the bolded words.

Make text italic

In order to make text italic, we use em element.This element also tells screen readers to emphasize italic words.
